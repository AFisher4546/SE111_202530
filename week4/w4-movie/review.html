Review of Five Nights at Freddy's

Andrew Fisher April 14th, 2025 ★★★★★

Check out the official IMDB page here.

Five Nights at Freddy's is an absolute phemonal roller coaster of emotions, perfectly reflecting the game series onto the big screen. The pure suspense, genuine fear, mixed together with occasional humor is just perfect, and references to the game series and community is icing on the cake. With the character choice, canon animatronic designs, and plot that will leave you on the edge of your seat, Five Nights at Freddy's is well worth the watch.

Trivia

  • YouTuber "MatPat" appears as a waiter named Ness, even says his signature catchphrase "It's just a theory".
  • One day, during recording, Director Emma Tammi heard someone calmly and quietly say "Foxy's arm is on fire".
  • The Toreador Song plays during a training video, a song heard when Freddy confronts you during a power outage in the first Five Nights at Freddy's game.
